===========================================
FINAL CONFIGURATION STEPS
===========================================

Wait for both deployments to finish (5-10 minutes total)

Then complete these 3 final steps:

---

STEP 1: GET YOUR VERCEL URL
----------------------------

Once Vercel finishes deploying:
1. Look for your frontend URL
2. It will be something like: https://pdf-ecourse-frontend-xxxx.vercel.app
3. Copy this URL

---

STEP 2: UPDATE CORS IN RENDER
------------------------------

1. Go to Render dashboard
2. Click on your "pdf-ecourse-backend" service
3. Click "Environment" in left sidebar
4. Find "CORS_ORIGINS" variable
5. Click "Edit"
6. Change value to:
   http://localhost:3000,https://YOUR-VERCEL-URL

   Example:
   http://localhost:3000,https://pdf-ecourse-frontend-xxxx.vercel.app

7. Click "Save Changes"
8. Wait 2-3 minutes for automatic redeploy

---

STEP 3: UPDATE SUPABASE REDIRECTS
----------------------------------

1. Go to Supabase dashboard
2. Click "Authentication" in left sidebar
3. Click "URL Configuration"
4. Under "Redirect URLs", add:
   https://YOUR-VERCEL-URL/auth/callback

   Example:
   https://pdf-ecourse-frontend-xxxx.vercel.app/auth/callback

5. Click "Save"

---

DONE! TEST YOUR APP
-------------------

1. Go to your Vercel URL
2. Click "Get Started" or "Log in"
3. Sign up with email/password
4. Upload a PDF (5-10 pages)
5. Wait for course generation (20-40 seconds)
6. Explore your course!

---

YOUR LIVE URLS:
---------------

Frontend: https://[your-vercel-url].vercel.app
Backend: https://pdf-ecourse-backend.onrender.com
GitHub: https://github.com/Raghuvarun93/pdf-ecourse-platform

---

TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------

If login doesn't work:
- Check Supabase redirect URL is correct
- Make sure it includes /auth/callback

If PDF upload fails:
- Check Render backend logs
- Verify GROQ_API_KEY is correct
- Wait a bit - free tier spins down after inactivity

If course generation is slow:
- Normal on free tier (20-60 seconds)
- Groq free tier rate limits apply
